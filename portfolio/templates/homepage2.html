ABOUT ME

Thanks for visiting my portfolio! My name is Kenny Maness, and I’m a software programmer & audio engineer. My software experience started with a coding bootcamp program through the nonprofit organization LaunchCode in St. Louis, Missouri. During the bootcamp, I gained a foundation in Python and Java - and then the pandemic hit, and I went back to my audio engineer roots until an opportunity for a Developer Apprenticeship popped up. I gained some more skills in that time - I’m a constant learner who loves languages (just ask me about my fluency in Swedish). More recently, I learned JavaScript and Node.js. - look out for some projects soon!

I recently completed a contract with Charter Communications, and I’m currently looking for my next step. To show you what you’d get from hiring me, I built a portfolio that’s also my website. If you opened the hood to my website, you would see a Django app hosted on an Nginx server in a Raspberry Pi. Some fun facts about me: My partner and I live in St. Louis with our little dog Bento. We have a board game collection that seems to grow on its own. On weekends, you can find us kayaking, hosting D&D in our basement board game den, or strolling leisurely through IKEA.
